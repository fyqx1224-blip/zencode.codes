Our Calculation Standards

Many BaZi tools contain systematic errors — Month Pillar offsets, incorrect Nà Yīn (納音 resonance tones), misaligned Twelve-Phase states. ZenCode has specifically corrected all known issues:

The Year Pillar 年柱 boundary is Lì Chūn 立春 (Start of Spring), not January 1st. The Month Pillar 月柱 changes at the monthly solar term 節, not the new moon. The Day Pillar 日柱 uses JDN with 甲子 Jiǎzǐ day as baseline. The Hour Pillar 時柱 correctly handles midnight 子時 Zǐshí day-rollover. Nà Yīn 納音 is computed using the 60-Jiǎzǐ cycle modulo method. Bone Weight 稱骨骨重 uses integer ×10 modulo to eliminate decimal precision errors.
